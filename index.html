Welcome to NewPascal!

NewPascal is a breath of fresh air and long tradition!

NewPascal exist to help both FPC/Lazarus and mORMot Open Source projects. This distribution is close to Ubuntu against Debian. Less conservative, but also less stable. Less platforms supported, but with latest features on main Linux/Windows targets.

Any patch include its own FPC_HAS... conditional define, to identify that we are running on a patched compiler, and that new features are available.

NewPascal works without installation. Just unpack zip archive, run configure.bat and next run.bat

We don't include any patch or unit from dubious source.
